TELANGANA

Telangana came into formation in the year 2014 on the second day of September and has a large number of tourist attractions that are visited by people from all over the world. The state has a rich historical and cultural heritage and has a large number of temples and monuments to prove so. The region has been under the rule of the Vakatas, the Chalukyas, the Satvahanas and later the Delhi Sultanate and the Nizams, and each of these has left a charming heritage and history that lures in travelers from far and wide.
